What is OSINT?

OSINT stands for Open-Source Intelligence, which is the process of collecting and analyzing publicly available information from a wide range of sources. This can include social media, websites, government reports, and more. OSINT is commonly used in cybersecurity, law enforcement, and intelligence gathering, but it can also be exploited for malicious purposes.

Illegal OSINT Practices
